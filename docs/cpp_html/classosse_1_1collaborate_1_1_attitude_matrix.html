Detailed Description

A 2D (3x3) matrix for reference frame and attitude transformations.

\[ {\bf{M}} = \begin{bmatrix} m_{0,0} & m_{0,1} & m_{0,2} \\ m_{1,0} & m_{1,1} & m_{1,2} \\ m_{2,0} & m_{2,1} & m_{2,2} \end{bmatrix} \]

\[ \bf{I} = \bf{M}^{-1} \]
