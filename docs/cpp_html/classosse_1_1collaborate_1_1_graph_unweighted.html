Detailed Description

Concrete graph with unweighted edges.

\[ n_x n_x \in \{True,False\} \]

\[ {\bf{E}} = Unweighted~Edges = \begin{bmatrix} 0 & n_0 n_1 & \dots & n_0 n_{N-1} & n_0 n_N \\ n_1 n_0 & 0 & \dots & n_1 n_{N-1} & n_1 n_N \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots & \vdots \\ n_{N-1} n_0 & n_{N-1} n_1 & \dots & 0 & n_{N-1} n_N \\ n_N n_0 & n_N n_1 & \dots & n_N n_{N-1} & 0 \end{bmatrix} \]
