Detailed Description

An attitude reference frame.

\[ \vec{x}_{axis}, \vec{y}_{axis}, \vec{z}_{axis} = \begin{bmatrix} x_x\\x_y\\x_z \end{bmatrix},~ \begin{bmatrix} y_x\\y_y\\y_z \end{bmatrix},~ \begin{bmatrix} z_x\\z_y\\z_z \end{bmatrix} ~~~(unit~vectors) \]

\[ {\bf{A}} = Attitude~AttitudeMatrix = \begin{bmatrix} a_{0,0} & a_{0,1} & a_{0,2} \\ a_{1,0} & a_{1,1} & a_{1,2} \\ a_{2,0} & a_{2,1} & a_{2,2} \end{bmatrix} \]
